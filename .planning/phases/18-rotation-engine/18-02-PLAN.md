  <action>
    Create `RotationService` with `generateSuggestions(mode: 'PURE' | 'POSITION' | 'CONSTRAINT')` method:
    - Inject `LiveGameStateService` and `PlaytimeService`.
    - **Step 1: Identify Candidates**
      - `benchPlayers`: All players currently on bench, sorted by `playtimeMap` (ascending - least played first).
      - `activePlayers`: All players currently on field, excluding Goalkeeper (slot index 0), sorted by `playtimeMap` (descending - most played first).
    - **Step 2: Matching**
      - **PURE mode:** Take the top $N$ bench players and pair them with the top $N$ field players in order.
      - **POSITION mode:** 
        1. Group field players by their current position name.
        2. Group bench players by their preferred position (or just match any if empty).
        3. Within each position group, match least-played bench with most-played field.
      - **CONSTRAINT mode:**
        1. Apply PURE matching logic, but filter candidates based on `rotationConfig`.
        2. Field player must have played at least $X$ continuous minutes (if `maxFieldMinutes` used as a proxy for "ready to sub out").
        3. Bench player must have rested at least `minBenchMinutes`.
        4. If no candidate meets constraints, fallback to PURE or return empty.
    - **Return:** `StagedSub[]` (array of {inPlayerId, outPlayerId}).
    - Create `rotation.service.spec.ts` with test cases for:
      - PURE rotation logic (least vs most played).
      - POSITION rotation logic (matching within groups).
      - CONSTRAINT logic (respecting bench/field limits).
      - Goalkeeper protection (GK never suggested for sub-out).
  </action>

  </verify>
